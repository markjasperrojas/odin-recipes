Adobong manok

Description

Adobong Manok, or Chicken Adobo, is a classic Filipino dish known for its savory and tangy flavor profile. It typically involves braising chicken in a marinade of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and black peppercorns. The dish is often simmered with bay leaves and sometimes includes other ingredients like onions and sugar. Adobong Manok is a versatile dish, enjoyed with rice and can be prepared with various cuts of chicken.

Key Ingredients and Flavors

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Black Peppercorns
  • Bay Leaves

Cooking Process

  1. Marinating:
    2.

    The chicken is marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and peppercorns.

  2. Braising:
    3.

    The marinated chicken is simmered in the marinade until cooked through and tender.

  3. Reduction:
    4.

    The sauce is often simmered until it thickens and develops a rich, glossy consistency.
