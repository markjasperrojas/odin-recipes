Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a comforting Filipino sour soup made with pork, shrimp, or fish and flavored with tamarind. It’s known for its tangy and savory broth, usually cooked with a mix of vegetables like kangkong, radish, eggplant, and long green beans. Sinigang is best enjoyed hot with steamed rice.

Key Ingredients and Flavors

  • Pork, shrimp, or fish
  • Tamarind (or sinigang mix)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Radish
  • Kangkong (water spinach)
  • Green beans

Cooking Process

  1. Sautéing:
    2.

    Start by sautéing onions and tomatoes to build the soup base.

  2. Boiling:
    3.

    Add meat or seafood and water, then bring to a boil until cooked and tender.

  3. Flavoring and simmering:
    4.

    Add tamarind or sinigang mix for the sour flavor, then simmer with vegetables until everything is cooked through.
